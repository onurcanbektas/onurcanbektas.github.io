As once Ayşe Berkman put it, "We are teaching how to use hammer to someone who does not know what a hammer is". She said this while talking about the problem of freshman students being drowned in the curriculum of mathematics education.

From my experience and observations, while teaching Mathematics in university level, there is almost no effort to give a motivation about the subject that is being taught, as if everyone knows why it is being taught.However, in reality, almost none of the freshman knows how the concept of a group is usefull, or what kind of beuty there is in the study groups and other algebraic structures, nor do they know why they are taught Calculus other than it is used in their Phyiscs classes.

Had I realied this earlier, instead of solving solving examples to understand the theory taught, I would read about to solve what kind of problems that the theory is developed
